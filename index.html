Dear Tanisha,

Happy Birthday. ❤️ I genuinely wish I was there right now, in person, to tell you that face to face. But since I can't be, I made you this instead.

Sometimes I think about how all of this started on November 27th, when you replied to my story. One small reply somehow turned into conversations throughout the day, coffee dates at Enjis, pickleball and badminton dates, avocado toasts, and you making pasta for me. Somewhere along the way, all those little moments started to mean a lot to me.

There are so many things I admire about you, Tanisha. The way you show up for the gym even when no one is watching. That quiet consistency says so much about who you are. The way you're fiercely independent and so comfortable being yourself. And the way you show up for the people you care about without hesitation. That's rare, and it's something I notice and appreciate more than you know.

Our first trip to Vrindavan on April 3rd, our countless coffee dates at Enjis, and you cooking pasta and sending it to me are the kinds of memories that randomly cross my mind and make me smile.

I hope today brings you everything you deserve, because you deserve so much happiness, laughter, and love. And selfishly, I just wish I could be there with you today.

Happy Birthday, Tanisha. ❤️ Thank you for being you. 🎂✨
